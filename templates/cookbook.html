Directions

Boil the pasta while frying chopped chicken thighs in butter. For the garlic bread, put bread with butter and garlic in the oven. Once the chicken is done, add chopped peppers, onions, red pepper flakes, tomatoes, spinach, and marsala wine. Once onions and peppers are fully cooked, add pasta, garlic, parmesan, and mozzarella. Serve with lettuce dressed with a bit of marsala wine and garlic bread.

Directions

Cook rice and set aside. In a wok, cook thinly sliced beef in vegetable oil and soy sauce. Remove. In the same wok, fry onions, garlic, and curry with sesame oil. Add rice to pan, frying in soy sauce and curry, adding more oil if needed. Chop lettuce and put in the bottom of the bowl, adding the fried rice on top, then beef, pickled cabbage, and sliced green peppers.

Directions
